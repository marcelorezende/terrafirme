Planned Expansions

Each expansion introduces a new biome or theme with its own Farm Modules, Innovations, and Challenges. Cards are scientifically grounded, community-shaped, and designed to layer onto the base game.

Booster ⚙️
Expansion 01
Tech Farmer

A booster pack of cutting-edge Innovations. Drones, AI soil sensors, vertical farms, gene editing, and the tech disrupting agriculture — for better or worse. Power up your farm or watch the unintended consequences unfold.

Precision Ag Drone Survey Vertical Farm AI Sensors Gene Editing
Booster 🌴
Expansion 02
Tropical Paradise

A booster of lush new Farm Modules inspired by rainforest ecosystems and indigenous farming wisdom. Shade-grown cacao, swidden agriculture, açaí agroforestry, and the extraordinary biodiversity of tropical communities who have farmed sustainably for millennia.

Cacao Forest Swidden Açaí Agro. Indigenous Canopy Layer
Full Edition 🌵
Expansion 03
Drylands

A complete 60-card standalone edition. Farming on the edge — from the Sahel to the Australian outback, the Negev to the Altiplano. Zaï pits, fog nets, the Great Green Wall, and the ingenuity of communities making arid land bloom.

Zaï Pits Fog Nets Great Green Wall Sand Dams 60 Cards

What’s Inside Every Expansion

Each pack follows the same structure as the base game, so it integrates seamlessly. Shuffle in the new cards and your next game night transforms.

🌾

Farm Modules

New land-use systems from the expansion's biome. Each generates PES credits in its own way.

💡

Innovations

Region-specific technologies and strategies that give you an edge or protect your farm.

🌊

Challenges

New environmental events that disrupt, force adaptation, or create unexpected alliances.

🥁

Community Cards

Cards designed by backers through the Card Creators programme, reviewed by the forester.

🌱 Shaped by the Community

Backers don't just fund expansions — they shape them. Vote on themes, submit card ideas through the Card Creators programme, and watch your contributions become part of the game. Every expansion includes at least one community-created card.

The Roadmap

Every expansion is funded by the community and released when it is ready — not rushed, not compromised. Here is where we are heading.

Now
Base Game
60 cards across three types. Farm Modules, Innovations, and Challenges. The foundation everything builds on.
Year 2 — 200+ Backers
Expansion 01: Tech Farmer (Booster)
The first booster pack. New Innovation cards bringing precision agriculture, drones, AI, and agtech disruption to the table.
Year 2–3
Expansion 02: Tropical Paradise (Booster)
New Farm Modules inspired by rainforest ecosystems and indigenous farming wisdom. Includes the first batch of Card Creator submissions.
Year 3
Expansion 03: Drylands (Full 60-Card Edition)
A complete standalone edition. Farming in arid regions worldwide — the Sahel, the outback, the Negev. The Grove reaches 500 surviving trees.
Year 3–4
The Future Farmer App
Digital version of the card game. Grove tracker. Agroforestry learning tools. The game goes global.
