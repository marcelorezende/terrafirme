The Creator

“I made The Future Farmer Card Game out of a love for nature and forests, years working in rural development, and a personal project to restore a small piece of land. I wanted a game that brought those worlds together — something fun to play with family and friends, but rooted in the real choices that farmers and land stewards face every day.”

Marcelo Rezende, Forester & Creator

The Mission

The Future Farmer Card Game is an educational card game rooted in real forestry and sustainable agriculture science. Built by a practicing forester, every card reflects real-world ecological dynamics. This authenticity is the core differentiator: genuine expertise wrapped in accessible, witty gameplay for ages 10 and up.
