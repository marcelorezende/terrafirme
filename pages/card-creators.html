How It Works

Every backer can submit card ideas. Quarterly, the top submissions are shortlisted and put to a community vote. The forester reviews voted cards for scientific accuracy, game balance, and ecological integrity.

1

Submit

Fill out the card form below with your idea, mechanic, and the real-world science behind it.

2

Review

The forester checks scientific accuracy, game balance, and ecological integrity.

3

Vote

Shortlisted cards go to a quarterly community vote among all backers.

4

Printed

Winning cards get professionally illustrated and permanently added to the game.
