THE METHOD — FOUR STEPS

01
Build the graph
Record species and processes as nodes; every observed interaction becomes a weighted edge.
02
Score centrality
Compute betweenness and degree to find which nodes hold the network together.
03
Rank priority
Cross centrality with vulnerability to rank nodes for investigation and intervention.
04
Plan restoration
Sequence interventions to rebuild the highest-value broken edges first.

Available in six languages.

The methodology document and node-scoring code are released openly so any group can map and restore their own ecoregion.
