01 — THE IDEA
POSITIONING

Every living thing is a node. Restoration is rebuilding the edges.

TerraFirme treats an ecosystem the way network science does: as a graph of species, plots and processes joined by measurable interactions. When a landscape degrades, nodes are lost and edges break. Our work is to assess the graph, monitor its change, and rebuild the connections that hold it together.

The identity makes that graph visible everywhere — the logo, the web platform, the card game and the field station are all the same network at different zoom levels. It sits above the Future Farmer as the credible, research-led parent brand.
