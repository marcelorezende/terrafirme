A.0 — CONCEPT

Firm ground, read like a core sample.

The identity treats every surface as a page of a survey atlas: layered earth, coordinate ticks, and a network of nodes planted on the ground line. Warm, exact, and unmistakably evidence-led.

B.0 — CONCEPT

The landscape as a living graph.

Direction B makes the ecosystem network the entire identity. Every node is a species, a plot, a relationship; every edge is a measured interaction. Brighter and more digital than the card game, it reads as a live instrument — the parent brand that the Future Farmer, the app, and the forum all plug into.
